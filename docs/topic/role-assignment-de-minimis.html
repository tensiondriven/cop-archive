Role Assignment "de minimis"

We're thinking thoughts and wondering if any orgs who have made a "de minimis" role assignment can provide a bit of context to the why/what prompted that type of assignment, and how it's worked out.

From GlassFrog - "You may exclude someone from representing their role in meetings only if (a) the role assignment is so minor it is "de minimis”, or (b) the role is multi-filled, and a circle policy limits representation to just one/some role-fillers. See Constitution §2.3 for details."
