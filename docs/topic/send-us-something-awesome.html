Send us something awesome :)

You may have heard that we're looking for awesome visual explanations of Holacracy and giving out free training seats to the winners.   

Thanks to your feedback, we’ve opened up the contest.  You don’t need to submit a file from a design program.  We invite you to write, draw, scribble, or collage your ideas about how Holacracy works onto a piece of paper and send us a picture! 

If you've got ideas, send 'em! Your brilliant, creative minds will help us to explain Holacracy to fellow practitioners and the Holacracy-curious.

You can email your submissions and questions to contest@holacracyone.com.

We can't wait to see what you send!
