Structure Statistics

Hello, community!  In "Beyond the Holacracy Hype," (link below), the authors said that Zappos employees, on average, fill 7.4 roles and are in 4.1 circles.  This made me curious about ARCA and others practicing Holacracy. 

Here's simple stats for ARCA:

Practicing since:  2014
# of Practitioners:  479
Average roles/person:  3.43
Median roles/person:  3
Average circles/person:  1.79
Median circles/person:  1

Anyone interested in sharing these numbers for other organizations?

Thanks!
