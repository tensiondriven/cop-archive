Hello everybody, I found a practice to ensure that people look to the accountabilities of roles and circles extensively. I ask for checklist items directly related to the accountabilities expressed in the role description. For example, if one accountability is "Update the pricing guidelines" I ask for a checklist item like "Pricing guidelines updated?".
