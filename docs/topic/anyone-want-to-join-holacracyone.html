Anyone want to join HolacracyOne?

Hey Folks,

We're looking for new team members to join HolacracyOne and help us spread a radical new way of organizing power.  Know anyone who might be interested? Send them our way!

In particular, right now we need someone with a deep background and passion in brand-building/marketing/outreach/PR to help lead our efforts in those areas, within our Outreach Circle.

We also really need someone who loves organizing stuff and communicating with people to fill roles like Spokesperson Assistant, Guest Experience, Participant Ambassador, and other communication and administrative/logistical roles.

Interested people can learn more and self-nominate via the form linked from this page:  http://www.holacracy.org/join-holacracyone

Thanks!
