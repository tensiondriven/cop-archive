How to get an index of everything on GlassFrog

So, there's lots of content on your GlassFrog. Some of it you know about - but what about the stuff you don't know about? IF only there was an index.

Well, there is!

Just type a space into the search bar and hit enter and you get a list of everything that's in there or edit this link:

https://app.glassfrog.com/search?utf8=✓&organization_id=[YOUR ORGANISATION ID]&search=+
