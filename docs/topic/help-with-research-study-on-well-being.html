Help With Research Study on Well-being

I would like to thank the administrators of Holacracy Community of Practice who have given me permission to post the following:

My name is Ben Olmos. I am a doctoral student at Northcentral University. I am in my final week of data collection for a research study I am doing for my dissertation, which measures the well-being of workers in a matrix versus Holacracy. 

I am seeking 92 participants for my research study with current or recent experience working in either a cross functional team with shared leadership or leadership using a traditional business hierarchy. Participants need to be over the age of 18, fluent in English, and have the ability to complete an online survey. To participate in this survey or for information regarding the purpose or procedures of this research study please go to https://goo.gl/forms/kINp42jlD3uefAiL2.
