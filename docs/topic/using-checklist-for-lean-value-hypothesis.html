Using Checklist for Lean Value Hypothesis

Reviewing Chris Cowan's Checklist Blog, I wanted to use Checklist to track Value, Growth, and Impact Hypotheses. I "believe" this does not violate usage of using checklist in this way, but I am open for critique. You can go on glassfrog and check out the hypotheses that I created. https://app.glassfrog.com/circles/1696#_checklist . One of the reasons why I wanted to do this as a way to check the feasible of our critical assumptions (that can make or break our current direction) before committing lots of time and money to something that is not feasible. To check this on a weekly basis, I thought, was a wise thing to do.
