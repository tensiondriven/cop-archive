It provides you with a view why Holacracy is a fully functioning alternative to the management hierarchy. What? This half-day workshop includes a simulation (in which you participate) of both a Holacracy style Tactical as well as a Governance meeting to give you a first-hand experience on how to structure, govern, and run an organization.
