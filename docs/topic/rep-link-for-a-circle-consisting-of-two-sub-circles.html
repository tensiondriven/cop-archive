Rep-link for a circle consisting of two sub-circles

We have a circle that includes 2 sub-circles consists which differ in the type of work, purposes and expectations.
Each sub-circle consists of 3 people and 5 roles (lead-link, rep-link, secretary, facilitator, programmer).
There are no other roles in this circle. (see image http://pastenow.ru/1O3O4)

Who can be a candidate for the role of a rep-link for this circle, so that he represents the interests of this circle in the Anchor circle?

If we choose a member of one of the sub-circles as a rep-link, can he effectively represent the interests of another sub-circle?

If we abandon the role of the rep-link in the Circle, then how can we endure the problems of sub-circles in the Anchor Circle?

Thank you

Hi Nadir,

Except the Lead Link, any core member of the Broad Circle may be elected as Rep Link of this Circle to the Anchor Circle.

He/She will "represent the interests" of the Broad Circle in the Anchor Circle, not the interests of the Sub-Circle 1 and the Sub-Circle 2, which are already represented in the Broad Circle though RL1 and RL2.

Hope that helps.
