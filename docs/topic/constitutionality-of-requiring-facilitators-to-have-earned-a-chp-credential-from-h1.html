Constitutionality of requiring Facilitators to have earned a CHP credential from H1

We've noticed in our organization that the quality of facilitators who have attended a training event from HolacracyOne is so far greater than the quality of those who haven't, we are considering a policy of requiring Facilitators in all circles to have earned a CHP credential from H1.

However, it seems to me like Policies are not able to modify the Governance process, which elections are part of.

Does that match your understanding?  Would the only way to enact such a change be for the Ratifier to amend the constitution as practiced within the organization?  Is that a common or accepted practice?
