Podcast on practicing Holacracy with Business School Lausanne

We are happy to share a 15-min podcast on the Holacracy journey of our team here at Business School Lausanne in Switzerland:

https://soundcloud.com/busines.../voicesofbslepisode9

The podcast is engaging and introspective and it reveals more about the initial challenges and possibilities that opened up as a result of introducing Holacracy at BSL in September 2015. See more about our experience of stumbling forward as Holacracy practitioners on our Blog where we regularly post about this exciting journey: http://bsl-blog.org/category/holacracy/

So proud to be the first educational institution globally to embrace Holacracy!
