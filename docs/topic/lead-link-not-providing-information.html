Lead link not providing information

In our Facilitator practice group we encountered the following. A Partner has a tension that he needs strategic information from the Super Circle to fulfill the purpose of one of his roles. As the Lead Link is not providing this info the Partner wanted to add an Accountability to the Lead Link that he has to provide transparency around the Strategy.

As Glassfrog, in line with article 2.2.3, does not provide the possibility to add Accountabilities to the Lead Link role we were wondering how to solve this tension.

Our ideas are:

  • Bring the tension to Tactical and ask for a metric where the LL will report on the Strategy.
  • Ask the Rep Link to bring the tension to the Super Circle and process it there with the Super Circle LL and the Circle LL.
  • Address the LL of the Super Circle and ask him to discuss this with the Circle’s LL. (With this solution it is not set in Governance so how can you then safeguard this for the longer term?)

 

Your insights on how to handle this tension will be much appreciated by us.

Hi Tom,

“A Partner has a tension that he needs strategic information from the Super Circle to fulfill the purpose of one of his roles” is a constraint that limits the Sub-Circle to be removed within the Super-Circle, which is the exact accountability of the Rep Link.

However, I'd try to solve the tension directly in the Sub-Circle instead of escalating the issue, in order to build a more “ autonomous” option. Note that "Bringing information to the circle” is not a LL accountability". Consequently, LL is not due to give information... so may be there is a lack of role somewhere.

For instance, you may create in the Sub-Circle a "Strategic information" role whose accountability could be "Bringing all strategic information needed by the circle"... or even directly add this accountability to the Rep Link Role.
