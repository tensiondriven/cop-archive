Holacracy Vocab: Circles -> Teams?

From how it works:

Holacracy replaces the traditional hierarchy with a series of interconnected but autonomous teams (“circles”).

If a new word adds some nuance not present in a common word, then it's an important thing to keep; if it adds nothing but another word to learn, it's better to stick with a more common word.

Is there any reason we couldn't/shouldn't just replace "circles" with "teams"?
