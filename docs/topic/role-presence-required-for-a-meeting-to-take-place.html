Role presence required for a meeting to take place ?

Q : Can I propose a policy "No governance meeting can be held without having at least 1 Role X present". Role X being a multi-filled role. My A : You could create a policy to require a quorum, but you cannot require a specific role to be present for the meeting to take place. Section 3.3.2 . Furthermore it would create a possible blocking situation (If the partners filling the role don't want to come, there's no meeting). Am I getting this right ?
