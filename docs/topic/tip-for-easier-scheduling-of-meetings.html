Tip for easier scheduling of meetings

Scheduling meetings - A great shortcut for sending out a calendar invite for core meetings is in GlassFrog to go to the page for your circle, and on the "Members" tab ctrl+click on the "Email Core Members" and select "Copy Email Addresses". You can then paste this into the invite in Google Calendar. (If it's important that certain people attend you may find the "Find A Time" tab in the web interface with Google Calendar useful.
