Share your work at the Holacracy Forum!

The Holacracy Forum team would love your help rounding out the lineup for the 2018 Forum.

We're still looking for exceptional speakers with something unique to share. If you or your colleagues have devised a new approach, developed an "app," or discovered something that will benefit other practitioners, consider sharing it at the Forum.

You could share by offering a talk or workshop during the main session, or hosting a conversation as part of the “open space" breakout session.

We’d especially love to see conversations on the following topics:

“Apps” for Common Business Functions

  • Hiring and firing
  • Performance review / Role fit
  • Budgeting and spending
  • Feedback Mechanisms
  • People & Culture
  • Resolving interpersonal, cultural or other challenges arising in the "people space"

Adoption
Methods for simplifying Holacracy adoption, successfully scaling it, or easing the "culture shock"

Facilitation & Coaching
Tools for improving facilitation or coaching

Organizational Purpose
Approaches to finding and evolving organizational purpose

Apply to Speak at Main Session - For presentations or workshops on practical tools or best practices related to Holacracy®.
Apply to Host a Breakout Session - For Open Space conversations about a challenge you've faced related to Holacracy®.

Speaker applications close April 30, 2018.

Thank you!
