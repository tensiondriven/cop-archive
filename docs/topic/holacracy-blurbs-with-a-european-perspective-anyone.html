Holacracy Blurbs with a European Perspective, anyone?

Dear friends, with the purpose of promoting the holacracy book in its italian translation I am aiming at collecting additional blurbs (in the same style of those you find on the back of the american edition as produced by Tony Hsieh and Evan Williams) that are somewhat relevant to the European market.

underlying Tension: Due to a lower  level of awareness on holacracy and self management in general, Italy  still thinks that it is mostly an american phenomenon, which usually implies skepticism about its fit to our contexts.

We all know that nowadays holacracy is far more than an experiment in several european countries (France, Netherlands, Austria, Switzerland, Poland, Germany,...)  and it would be great to collect  blurbs from european decision makers of some importance.

Therefore if you already know of the existence of messages of this type, or know of clients of relevant scope / size / visibility, who would be interested in being mentioned please forward them to me by either pointing to existing weblinks where these blurbs already exist (in response to this topic), or by sending me info here: andrea.fare@leapfrog.team

feedback on alternative pathways to solve my tension are welcome!

 Ciao.

Andrea.
