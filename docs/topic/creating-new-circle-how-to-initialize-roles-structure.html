      Hi Brian,

      A way is to hold a private "set-up gov meeting" with the (future) Lead Link, in order to describe the minimum of roles (on going activities) needed to reach the purpose of the new circle. Captur the initial structure using GlassFrog.

      After that, improve this initial structure tension driven through regular Gov meeting process.

      Hope that helps,
