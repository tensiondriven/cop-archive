Holacracy Brazillian Community

Hello, guys! I'm excited to announce that we are creating probably the first Holacracy Brazillian community

Right now, we have a meetup (www.meetup.com/pt/holacraciabrasil/) and a website/blog (www.holacraciabrasil.com).

In the website, we intend to publish:

  • Implementation stories;
  • Translations of allowed official materials (respecting the licenses);
  • Articles and other materials with our interpretation of the Constitution, best practices, experiences, and more.


So, please let me know (email me) if you want to participate or help with some content
