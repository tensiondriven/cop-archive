Subject
Holacracy Coach Training - Amsterdam
Extract
Guiding others through a Holacracy adoption requires a solid Holacracy practice, a firm grasp on the rules of the Constitution, and a toolbox for coaching others through a radical behavioral and mindset shift. The Holacracy Coach training will deepen your foundation of Holacracy knowledge, and help you develop the skills to guide others through the transition to self-management. This training is designed for participants seeking to build on learnings from the Holacracy Practitioner Training.
