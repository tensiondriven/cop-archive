Who can fill Elected Roles?

I have a question concerning the election process of the Facilitator. In section 2.5.1 of the Constitution stays “[…] All Core Circle Members are eligible for election […]”. Does that mean that Partner from outside the circle can’t be a candidate for the Facilitator and can't fill this role?

Tom Mulder

[@mention:553141978014385339]. That is according to 2.5.1 but the Lead Link may according to 2.3.4. appoint additional persons to serve as a Core Circle Member. This person can then be elected as the Facilitator for the Circle.

So there can be Partners from outside the Circle as a Facilitator.

[@mention:553141978014385339]. I would keep the election process as is in the Constitution. The Lead Link can inform the Circle upfront that an outside Partner will be part of the Core Cirlce members.

All the rules in the Constitution then apply as for all Circle Members. This person can then be elected like all Circle Members.
