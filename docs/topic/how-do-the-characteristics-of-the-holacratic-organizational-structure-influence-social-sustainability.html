How do the characteristics of the Holacratic Organizational Structure influence Social Sustainability

Hello all,

My team is studying a Masters program in Strategic Leadership Towards Sustainability at the Blekinge Institute of Technology in Karlskrona, Sweden. Our area of research is centered on How characteristics of the Holacratic Organizational Structure influence Social Sustainability. As such, we want to hear from the people using it. If you are a Holacracy practitioner or work in an organization using Holacracy, we would really appreciate and value your participation in our brief survey here: http://www.surveymonkey.com/r/holacracysurvey

If you know someone working with Holacracy that might be interested in our study, please share this link with them: https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/holacracysurvey

I am happy to chat more about our research or answer any questions you might have.
