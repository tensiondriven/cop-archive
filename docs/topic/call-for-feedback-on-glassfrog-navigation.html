Call for feedback on GlassFrog navigation

Hello Practitioners,

I'm Jonathan, coming to you in the UX Designer role at HolacracyOne.  We recently pushed out some changes to the navigation system used in GlassFrog.  After processing some feedback from users, we decided to revert these changes for now, until we can address the tensions that arose from the change.

If you had a reaction to the recent change, I'd like to hear from you.

As GlassFrog users, I'm interested in your feedback on the following:

- When teaching new people GlassFrog, what do you tell them with regard to navigation?

- What do you like or dislike about the current navigation?

- What could be improved?
