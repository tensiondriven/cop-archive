Can a Policy demand action?

From 1.4 Authority over Domains You may also define “Policies” for your Domains, which are either grants of authority that allow others to control or cause a material impact within a Domain, or limits on how others may do so when otherwise authorized . My current interpretation of this is that a Policy can't demand action.
