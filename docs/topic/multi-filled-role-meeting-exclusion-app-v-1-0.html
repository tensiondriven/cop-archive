Multi-filled Role Meeting Exclusion App v. 1.0

Too many people in your meetings? 

Propose this Policy:

"As per article 2.3.2 of the constitution, when multiple people are assigned to the roles of [Role that has too many role-fillers], then only one of the role-fillers is considered a Core Circle Member (and thus invited to the circle meetings). This role-filler will represent the Role during the Circle's meetings.

This role-filler is assigned by the Lead Link of the circle, and has a duty to consider and process Tensions conveyed by the excluded role-fillers, exactly as a Rep Link would were the Role a Sub-Circle."
