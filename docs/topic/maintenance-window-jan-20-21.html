Maintenance Window Update - Jan 20/21

Hey everyone! Update on the maintenance window - everything went suspiciously well last night, and GlassFrog was back up for us by 3am EST/9am CET and we're seeing that it is up across the world. But we've gotten reports from a few folks that they are still seeing the maintenance page with the lily pads. If you're seeing that page still, your hosting providers are probably still  updating their records.

If you're still seeing the page, let us know. We expect that most folks are up now, but technically, it can take 24-48 hours for some ISPs to get their records updated. GlassFrog is up and happily hopping along, so you may still see emails from activity by others in your org (though, hopefully they're having a restful weekend!).
