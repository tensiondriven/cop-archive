Public "Profile"

I have a dream ... :-)

Like a lot of people using GlassFrog, I started, a long time ago, to add a link to my role in my email signature.

Works perfectly for my colleague having an account in GlassFrog on my organization. But as our governance is not public, does not work for anyone else (inside or outside my company)

A shame :-( 

My dream would be then : could we imagine a "public" profile in GlassFrog ?

Same content as the one we have when looking at a partner, with all the details (circles, roles), but without the actual links to the components (the would require the governance to be public).

What do you think ? Anyone else would be interested ?
