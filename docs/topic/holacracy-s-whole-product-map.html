Holacracy's Whole Product Map

As Whole Product Manager , I want to share my current model for Holacracy's "whole product." If you're unfamiliar with that term, you're likely already familiar with the concept. "Whole product" means everything you need to augment the "core product" to really make it work. It comes from Geoffrey Moore's book Crossing the Chasm as a way to explain a product's entire ecosystem.
