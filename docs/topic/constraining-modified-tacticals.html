Constraining modified tacticals

Scenario:

- tactical is modified to be "merged" with a meeting format that has specific time constraint (i.e. start time), reason for merging is that being that second type of meeting a good source for tactical tensions, putting the two together makes sense.

- the policy at play includes the start time as a constraint (tactical is modified with steps x,y,z and must start at xx:xx)

Question :

Your opinion? Would you strike it/amend it/fix it  in any way and for what reason? (on one hand the explicit time impacts the scheduling rights of the secretary and should probably be modified into something that leaves room for his judgement on the other hand it's a modified tactical...)
