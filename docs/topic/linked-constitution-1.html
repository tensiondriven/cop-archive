Linked Constitution

I would love to see a Constitution that dynamically links the threads from this CoP to the relevant sections and subsections of the Constitution. This could allow for a deep dive into the shared collective wisdom of the CoP when seeking clarity or to interpret certain sections. A hit-count and rating system beside each subsection could surface those elements where revision is most needed. Of course, tying the CoP comments to the subsections and keeping the member's certification and roles in mind (by integrating with GlassFrog) would also help develop the training program and practicioner training/coaching -- while increasing GF's importance.
