Hi Cathy

We managed to re-certify our ISO9001-cerfiticate after one year of pure holacracy-practice. We could even manage to get ISO27001 information security certification which is much harder.

Also as Luca mentioned showing all the roles, circles and their accountabilites help a lot. For the rest we could show at the audit that people following the defined processes in the management system (as before Holacracy).

The approach was to take our "old" management-system and forge it to one that was lead by the spirit and rules of Holacracy. We tried not to get into a situation with two "systems" beeing incompatible or worse fighting each other

So far it went well - although a complete fusion will take years i suppose because the character of the two systems is completely different...
