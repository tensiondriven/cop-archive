I have a problem with Holocracy. A business is a system, and as such it has one or very few constraining elements that limit achieving whatever its goal is. Current organisational structures are not good at finding and exploiting the constraining element(s) properly – because management gives every part its target – targets are often in conflict and local departments will optimise themselves … causing damage to the constraining element.
