Dedicated vertical scroll for Agenda item list

We have usually between 20 and 30 items in our Tactical meetings. So around the 10th item, the secretary starts scrolling down to select the item on the list (left part of the window), and scroll up to go back to the project/action text box on the right part.

Would it be feasible to add a dedicated scroll bar for the item list ? Or another way could be than closed items are sent to the bottom of the list, so the next item is always on the top ?

Many possibilities I guess, the idea being that the Project/Action text boxes always stay on sight, so is the current item.
