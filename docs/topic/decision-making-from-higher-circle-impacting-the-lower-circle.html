Decision making from higher circle impacting the lower circle

What if higher circle takes a decision which creates an accountability on the lower circle. Then will the lead link  of the lower circle take the tension  to the lower circles governance meeting and create an agenda in governance of the lower circle and create the new role based on the policy of the higher circle. Same way if the lower circle decides which creates a tension which can be solved by creating an accountability or a policy in the higher circle, then would the rep link take this tension and propose a new policy or role or accountability in the higher circle.
