Hi Shammi!

shammi nanda posted:

we have a compensation designer role with this purpose and accountabilities - https://app.glassfrog.com/roles/11188478

If i in that role bring a compensation policy which also needs inputs from other roles, then do i need to bring it to governance? 

 

To bring in the Policy itself, the Role holder does not need to use the Governance process: they already have the relevant Domain to work with.

However, as long as the other Roles do not have Accountabilities to provide input on the Policy (or the process that the Policy specifies), the compensation designer cannot expect them to do this work.

Therefore it may be helpful to propose Governance that adds Accountabilities to the other Roles.
