Hi Adam,

One big step forward of Holacracy is to separate Tactical from Governance meetings.

Mixing them together would move backward the both processes.

Another perspective is that Gov process is using IDM to set circle rules, in order to allow people being totally autocratic in there roles. As most the rules are clear, most people may be free.

Last point is the governance is "working on the organisation", wich is not so frequent... and totaly different from "working in the organisation".
