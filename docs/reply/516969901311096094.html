I've looked for a more relevant definition of Triage to help you (best what I could find: http://whatis.techtarget.com/definition/triage) but i think an exact definition is not going to give the answer.

I think triage in this context means: there is a tension, and you decide what needs to be done to reduces this: a next-action, a project or something else. Like with the patient (triage in medical terms), you assess what's needed and process the tension in the most suitable way.  

So I think your understanding of the term triage is correct (for what it's worth as I'm not very experienced in Holacracy yet)
