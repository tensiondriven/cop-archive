How does your organisation budget?
User
Ruben @ Springest
Published
10/24/2015
Body
  • Approach: circle Lead Links request budget per project from a role Budgets & Resources that we created because we felt the general fall back to the super circle LL was too implicit / unclear. On top of that there are some predefined / rolling budgets
  • Tools: Some are documented in Asana under the relevant projects, some are in our Google Apps sheets coupled with the rest of our forecasting
  • Tensions: none since they would be solved immediately in governance (which most circles do weekly here). There has been tension about regularly requesting budgets, fixed by predefining them. I think we could do a better job of coupling budgets to roles, this is currently partly implicit.
  • Toolset: can you give an example, not sure I understand the question.
