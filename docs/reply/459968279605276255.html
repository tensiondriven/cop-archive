Tactical Meetings would get really confusing if Governance changed while they are running!  It's good practise to let Governance changes "sink in" and then collect Tactical Tensions. This gives good grounding.

 

We also follow the process that Ruben outlined and sometimes run Tactical and Governance meetings after another, beginning with the Tactical.
