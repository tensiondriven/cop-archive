Davi Gabriel da Silva posted:

The Constitution says that the Secretary is accountable for:

  • Capturing and publishing the outputs of the Circle’s required meetings, and maintaining a compiled view of the Circle’s current Governance, checklist items, and metrics


A policy change is a possible output of a Governance meeting, and this accountability states that the Secretary must publish these outputs. So it depends on your interpretation about what "publishing" is.

 

That's a really good point - thanks for this!
