Purely Poultry has used Patrick Lencioni's method described in The Advantage: Why Organizational Health Trumps Everything Else in Business pages 82-87. It definitely comes assuming structure already exists in the organization which is part of what lead us to Holacracy. Their section on Create Clarity has 6 critical questions one of which is "Why do we exist?"  

We have a One Page Playbook Policy and an Organizational Health Meeting Policy

HolacracyOne has a Purpose Guide role that we have mimicked.
